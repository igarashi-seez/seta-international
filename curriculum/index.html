Curriculum

We have given great thought to our curriculum and the organization of our classes. Our goal is to provide your child a balanced and complete educational program in a family-like and well equipped environment. Our school program is designed to nurture children of all linguistic backgrounds through an appropriate program tailored to the particular stage of your child’s development.

We focus our curriculum on the physical, cognitive, emotional, social and creative needs of each child. Seta International School is a place where children are given the opportunity to learn and grow as whole individuals. Our school activities address the needs of the total child.

The school is designed to encourage and guide children in the development of self-discovery, individual responsibility and intellectual growth. We believe that children can reach their full potential if they are able to develop a positive self-image.

To emphasize this goal, our school offers an environment in which they can learn to choose their activities, cooperate, share and form friendships with peers. We emphasize helpfulness, fairness, willingness to use one’s abilities for the benefit of others, and the importance of being a global citizen who cares for the environment and other cultures.
