Admissions

Thank you for your interest in Seta International School. We have rolling admissions and accept applications throughout the year.

Admissions Process

1.Visit us for a school tour.

If you’re interested in learning more about our curriculum and programs, you are invited to visit us for a school tour, although it is not mandatory. To schedule your tour, please fill out the online school tour request form below. We look forward to meeting you.

School Tour request formリクエストフォームはこちら

2.Submit your application.

When you are ready, submit your application form for enrollment. Please click on the image below to download a copy of the application form. The form is also included in the information package you may receive during a tour.

Preschool & Kindergarten Application Form

Once completed, the form should be scanned and emailed to sip@seta-inter.com or sent via regular mail to:
Seta International School
Attention: Admissions
1-27-12 Seta, Setagaya, Tokyo 158-0095
There is no application fee when submitting application forms.

3.Complete the registration.

Upon receipt of a completed application, an invoice of the initial fees will be issued. With confirmation of the payment, the registration of your child’s enrollment has been completed.

Assessment

The school will contact prospective Pre-Kindergarten and Kindergarten students, if necessary, to conduct an assessment to determine if the program of each class is able to meet the needs of the student.

If you have any questions or needs assistance regarding the admissions, please contact us at 03-5717-6769 or at sip@seta-inter.com
