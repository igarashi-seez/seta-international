Summer School Age:2-8 year olds

*We are accepting the application to our summer school, however please note that there might be a possibility to not hold the program due to the current circumstances regarding COVID-19. We also may ask for your agreement regarding the health measures at school to participate in our summer program.

Seta International School offers a fun-filled summer program for children (ages 2-8) starting from July 4, and running until August 5, 2022.
The program is divided into several theme-based, one-week sessions where the children
will participate in various activities designed by our teachers. Please note that different
teams of Seta teachers will be coordinating the program during different sessions and
that substitute teachers may participate occasionally. The program welcomes both
children who are currently enrolled in regular classes at Seta and those who are not.

If you are interested in enrolling your child in this program, please submit an online application below. We will contact you to proceed with the registration. The registration will be completed with the confirmation of the required documents and payment. Please be advised that there is limited space available, so we can accommodate children on a first-come, first served basis. If you have questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at any time.
