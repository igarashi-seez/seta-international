Job Opportunity

Seta is always looking for great teachers to be part of our team! If you are interested in joining our team, please send us your resume with your picture to sip@seta-inter.com.

Required qualifications for applicants:

  • Must have at least AA degree (preferably BA degree) in Early Childhood Education or another related field.
  • Must have at least 2-3 years of experience working with young children (2 to 6 years old).
  • Native English speaker.
  • Willingness to learn our school curriculum through training.
  • Plan to stay in Japan longer than two years.
  • Great at working as a part of a team.
  • Also, the applicant must be able to come in for an interview in person.
*Please note that you will be a teacher's assistant during the first year, which will also be your training period.
