Health & Safety

The students' health and safety is our top priority at Seta International School. We especially talk about the importance of health during our annual Health Weeks when a dentist and a nurse visit our school. We also conduct evacuation drills due to living in a highly seismic country.
Food allergies are a growing concern in schools around the world, and we have many children with food allergies at our school. As we value cooking activities, we try our best to keep the food in the activities allergy free. In order to provide everyone with a safe environment, we may ask for your support by not bringing certain foods to school or school events.
In addition, our staff members are trained in CPR. First aid equipment is available at the school. Any serious injuries will be treated at a local hospital, which is a 5-minute drive away from school.
A field trip to the local fire station is another element in our safety learning. This gives the children an opportunity to meet the fire fighters, see the fire engines and an ambulance closely, and tryout their uniforms and the equipment.

Community

Parent Involvement

A parents' role in his/her child's education is very important. As Seta International School, parent-teacher conferences are held twice a year to share our observations and discuss each child's development and ways to guide them to reach individual goals. We also encourage parents to participate in our school's special events, such as concerts, graduations, potluck parties, field trips, and our monthly joint birthday parties when parents can help celebrate their child's special day with classmates.

Alumni

Whether you attended Seta for just for one year or five, you are an important member of the Seta's Alumni Family. We are excited for your successes and encourage you to stay connected with us.
Do you have news you would like to share with your classmates? If you moved, had an exciting outing, won an award, or just want to stop by, we would like to know! We look forward to sharing your news with the Seta family. You are also more than welcome to come back to school as a guest speaker.
Gathering events are occasionally held when alumni can meet and catch up with old friends, classmates and teachers to relish and refresh on past memories.
